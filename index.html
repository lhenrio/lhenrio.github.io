Research interests:

Semantics, Object calculi, Components, Concurrency and Distribution, Confluence and Determinacy, Static analysis, Java, Java Card, model checking, theorem proving, fault tolerance, distributed systems.

My research topics include the development of formal aspects of distributed objects and components, like for example the ASP calculus. I applied those tools  to various topics as confluence, fault tolerance, ...
I also did a few research on static analysis in different contexts and am also interested in behavioural specification of objects and components.

Fairy School —A magical board game

Characteristics

  • 2 to 5 players or more
  • approx. 100 min, 75 minutes for the short variant
  • parallel play
  • regular/exert players

Fairy school is a little magical.

  • It is a deckbuilding game but with a lot of control and planning on the cards played,
  • It is a competitive game but where players are incentised to collaborate with their neighbours,
  • It is a game with resources but resources are elements,
  • It plays in parallel and can thus be adapted to many players

The main orginality of the game is the importance given to positive interaction between players.
